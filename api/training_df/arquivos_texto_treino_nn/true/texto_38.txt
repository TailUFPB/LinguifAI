ruling by u s  district judge george daniels is the first to weigh the merits of the u s  constitution s anti corruption provisions as they apply to trump  a wealthy businessman who as president bring the suit  the plaintiffs included the nonprofit watchdog group citizens for responsibility and ethics in washington  crew   a hotel owner  a hotel events booker and a restaurant trade group  the
